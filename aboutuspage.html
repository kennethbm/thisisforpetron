About us

Petron Corporation is the largest oil company in the Philippines, operating the country's only remaining refinery. Also a leading player in the Malaysian market, we have a combined refining capacity of 268,000 barrels per day, producing a full range of world-class fuels and petrochemicals to fuel the lives of millions of Filipinos and Malaysians.

History

Petron traces its rich heritage to September 7, 1933 when Socony Vacuum Oil Company of New York and the Standard Oil Company of New Jersey merged to form the Standard Vacuum Oil Company or Stanvac. After suspending operations in World War II, Stanvac promptly resumed its operations in 1945 and helped rebuild a newly-independent nation.
